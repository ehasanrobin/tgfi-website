Privacy Policy

tgfi.com respects the confidentiality, integrity and security of your information. There is a severe commitment not to share them with third parties or use them improperly to send E-mail Marketing or SPAM. Communications made by e-mail are intended to keep you informed about the purchase and/or subscription of services. We emphasize that personal data will never be shared or disclosed to third parties, with exception when we are required by law to do so and/or to partners and suppliers (Wix) and sending email marketing. We will only have access to your registration data (name, email), passed on by the Wix platform and, if you want to completely remove your registration from our website, you can request it by email.
